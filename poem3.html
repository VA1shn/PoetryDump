Clown

Feeling like a clown, faking smiles and cracking jokes,
Acting crazy and happy but really, I’m left with no hopes.
All they see is something silly, all they see is someone funny,
But I'm just so tired of crying, so now all I can do is joke.

I’m nothing but a clown, face bright and smile painted,
Masked up as child’s play, but my innocence is tainted.
To all the clowns who stumble and fall just for laughs,
I hope you’ll be fine; I hope you don’t collapse.

In the grand scheme of things we made someone’s day better,
But is a smile on their faces worth our pain? Maybe, maybe not.
