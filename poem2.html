Chaos

The frantic thoughts
that get lost in translation,
The crazy thoughts
that question my sanity,
The happy thoughts
that have me smiling ear to ear,
And the sad thoughts
that make me feel hollow yet heavy,

The anger that
dissipates as fast as it comes,
And the regret that
i wish wasn't a fleeting memory,
The emotions i always
wished i could do away with,
They continue to
make me, shake me and break me.

My anxiety is a bitch,
it leaves me scared and tired,
And I still struggle
to keep my thoughts and emotions at bay,
But I'll take this chaos
over the silence anyday,
As I'm in love with this
imperfect version of me!
