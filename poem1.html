The tree speaks

Once upon a time,
everybody loved me.
They thought I was God,
And always respected me.
Slowly, everyone started
taking things from me;
They took my shade and flowers,
ate my fruits and filled their tummy.
But still they did love me,
they didn’t take everything.
But somehow, times changed,
And thought they were the king.
The humans became greedy;
They cut my wood to make things;
They started killing my friends,
When the fellows had only few rings.
But still I feel happy;
I am giving them a lot.
I just hope they realize,
What dangers they have brought.
I don’t mind giving them my wood;
I don’t mind being their cot;
I just hope they realize,
What dangers they have brought.
