Halloween!

Hi, Little Pumpkin, how have you been?
Rise and shine, it's Halloween!!

Smile, little pumpkin, nice and welcoming
to the group of shy people wandering.

Meek, curious ghosts will lurk around
muted in the pretty lights and sounds;

Little children won't cry today
and merry makers party away.

The night of ghosts and scary things
now belongs to the human beings!

The dark is lit with giddy smiles;
candy, chocolates, nothing vile.

An obscenity it has become,
reeking of whiskey and rum.

The colours and costumes are a joke,
Oh Festival of Darkness, have some hope!

Shine, Little Pumpkin, let the show begin,
Spread your terror, it's Halloween!!
